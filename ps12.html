Come here to play an adaptation of the Classic Tic Tac Toe

Rules:

  1. Red Moves first
  2. In each turn you can put your color on an empty(white) space
  3. A player wins by securing 3 squares in a row of his/her color. 
It can be vertically, horizontally, ordiagonally
  4. No more than 6 squares can be marked at a time.
So the seventh square to be marked opens up the earliest one that was marked.
